A word from the Chief Procurement Officer

Our global procurement organisation is located in 10 different locations around the world to support Maersk's vision of becoming the global integrator of container logistics, connecting and simplifying the global supply chain for our customers. We are determined to deliver value to the business and drive the cost leadership agenda by collaborating with our suppliers and stakeholders around innovation, new technology, and eliminating inefficiencies when interacting on the supply chain side or digitally. One of the targets we as a company have set ourselves is the ambitious goal of becoming carbon neutral by 2050.

Our suppliers will play an important part in us reaching that ambitious goal, meaning that our supplier collaboration will become even more important in jointly finding new innovative ideas and concepts to achieve that goal.

Our employees are the most important part in everything we do. We therefore continually invest in people development and give them a great sense of empowerment in what they do. We want to make sure that we have the right people and competencies in the organisation enabling them to take the relevant decisions when presented to them. We always strive to learn from the best in the industry. This also applies on the digital and technology side. We therefore collaborate across multiple sectors and use that network and input to optimise our processes and systems, applying robotics and AI in the way we operate. We are thereby consistently challenging status quo and striving to be at the forefront of what we do.

We aim at going All The Way in everything we do; whether it relates to our people, digital solutions or how we collaborate with suppliers.
